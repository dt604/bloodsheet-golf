# Privacy Policy for BloodSheet Golf

**Last Updated:** March 5, 2026

BloodSheet Golf ("we", "us", or "our") operates the BloodSheet Golf mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you:
- **Profile Data**: We collect your name, handicap, and email (via Supabase Auth) to manage your golf performance and match history.
- **Media**: If you choose to upload "Moments" (photos or videos), these are stored in our secure database for the duration of your match and profile.
- **Location**: We may request access to your location to help you find nearby golf courses and automate course selections.

## 2. Use of Data
BloodSheet Golf uses the collected data for various purposes:
- To provide and maintain the Service
- To notify you about changes to our Service
- To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Service (Live Scorecards, Trash Talk)
- To provide customer care and support
- To monitor the usage of the Service

## 3. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored securely using **Supabase** (PostgreSQL/Auth). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your "Blood Coin" balance is a virtual currency and has no real-world cash value.

## 4. Disclosure Of Data
We do not sell your personal data. We may disclose your data only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.
